## Komponente
`components/Skill.vue`

```vue
<template>
  <div class="Skill">
    <h3 class="Skill__title">{{ title }}</h3>
    <div class="Skill__background">
      <div class="Skill__level" :style="{ width: level + '%' }">
        {{ level }}%
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</template>
```
